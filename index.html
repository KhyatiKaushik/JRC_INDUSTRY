Our Products

Advanced defense ammunition and explosive materials

RDX (Cyclotrimethylenetrinitramine)

High-performance military explosive with superior stability and power. Used in various ammunition applications and demolition charges.

  • High explosive yield
  • Temperature stable
  • Military grade quality

HMX (Octogen)

Premium explosive material with exceptional performance characteristics. Ideal for specialized military applications requiring maximum efficiency.

  • Superior energy density
  • Thermal stability
  • Precision applications

PETN (Pentaerythritol tetranitrate)

High-velocity explosive compound used in detonating cord, shaped charges, and precision demolition applications.

  • High detonation velocity
  • Excellent sensitivity
  • Shaped charge applications

Hand Grenades

Complete range of tactical hand grenades for various military and law enforcement applications with reliable performance.

  • Fragmentation variants
  • Smoke & flash options
  • Safety mechanisms

Artillery Shells

Precision-manufactured artillery ammunition for various calibers and applications, meeting international military standards.

  • Multiple calibers
  • High accuracy
  • Quality assurance

Custom Solutions

Tailored ammunition solutions designed to meet specific operational requirements and specialized military applications.

  • Technical consultation
